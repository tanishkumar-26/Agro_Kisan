About Agro Kisan

Empowering farmers with technology-driven agricultural solutions

Our Story

Agro Kisan was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between traditional farming and modern technology. What started as a small initiative to help local farmers in Karnataka has now grown into a nationwide platform serving thousands of farmers across India.

Our journey began when our founder, Rajesh Patel, noticed his family struggling with unpredictable crop yields due to lack of access to real-time market prices and weather information. This inspired him to create a solution that would empower farmers with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions.

Our Mission

To provide farmers with accurate, timely information and tools that enhance productivity, reduce risks, and increase profitability in agriculture.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted agricultural platform, transforming farming practices through innovation and technology.
